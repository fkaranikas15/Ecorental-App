Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About the Bike Rental Booking Process on Our Website: Answers to Common Concerns and Inquiries.

1. How do I make a reservation for a bike rental?
Making a reservation is quick and easy. Simply visit our website or give us a call to check bike availability for your desired dates. You can then proceed to book online or provide the necessary details over the phone. Our team will confirm your reservation and ensure the bike is ready for your adventure.
2. What if I have a mechanical issue with the bike?
In the unlikely event of a mechanical issue, please contact us immediately. Our team will provide you with instructions on how to proceed. Depending on the situation, we may arrange for a replacement bike or offer on-site assistance to resolve the issue promptly, ensuring your ride continues smoothly.
3. Can I rent bike accessories such as helmets, locks, or baskets?
Absolutely! We offer a range of bike accessories available for rent to enhance your cycling experience. These include helmets for safety, locks to secure the bike, and baskets or racks for carrying personal belongings. Simply let us know your accessory requirements during the reservation process, and we'll ensure they are ready for you along with your bike.
